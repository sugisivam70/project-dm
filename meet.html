Meet Our Team

Mr. Dinesh K S

Founder, Partner

Mr. Dinesh K S is the Founder and Partner of DM Technologies, bringing over 20+ years of rich experience in the VMC and precision machined components industry. He has worked with reputed organizations such as Cimotech and AUMA India, gaining deep exposure to high-precision manufacturing, quality standards, and large-scale industrial operations.

A highly skilled CNC / VMC programmer and technical analyst, Mr. Dinesh is known for optimizing cycle times, improving productivity, and delivering cost-effective machining solutions without compromising quality. He holds a DMM Degree along with an ITI Diploma in Mechanical Engineering.

His expertise spans the complete manufacturing lifecycle—from design interpretation to final inspection. He has played a key role in critical defense manufacturing projects, including components for the Akash Missile System and Pinaka Multi-Barrel Rocket Launcher for Indian defense forces.

With hands-on experience in advanced international machining technologies, including JUARISTI 6-Axis CNC machines (Spain), he has handled large-scale components with workspaces up to X: 12 m, Y: 2 m, Z: 1.5 m. His work reflects a deep passion for precision engineering and continuous innovation.
